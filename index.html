Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose grafted fruit plants?

Grafted plants fruit earlier (2–3 years), deliver consistent, variety-faithful fruit, increase resistance to local pests/diseases and improve yield reliability — speeding your return on investment.

How soon will I see fruit?

With our grafted seedlings you can expect first harvests around 2–3 years depending on variety and farm management.

Where is Avocam located?

We operate from Village Bafou, Nkong-Ni, Menoua. We serve farmers across the Menoua department and can advise on varieties adapted to your land.
